# Client Follow-Up

The difference between a thriving freelance business and a struggling one usually comes down to follow-up. But when you're juggling 20+ clients, remembering who you promised to "circle back with next week" is nearly impossible. Client Follow-Up tracks every client interaction, reminds you when it's time to reach out, and can even draft personalized follow-up emails that sound like you wrote them yourself.

## What It Does

- Tracks all client interactions across email and calendar, building a timeline for each relationship
- Sends or drafts personalized follow-up messages at intervals you set — after proposals, meetings, project completions, or just periodic check-ins
- Gives you a daily "who needs attention" view so no relationship goes cold

## How It Works

1. Connect your email and import your client list (or let the agent build one from your inbox).
2. Set follow-up rules: how soon after a meeting, how often for existing clients, when to nudge dormant relationships.
3. Review the daily follow-up queue — approve, edit, or let auto-send handle it.

## Works With

- Gmail, Outlook
- Google Contacts, Calendly
- Slack, LinkedIn
- Notion
