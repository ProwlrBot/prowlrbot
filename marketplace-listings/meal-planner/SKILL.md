# Meal Planner

The "what's for dinner?" question costs you more than you think — decision fatigue, impulse grocery runs, expensive takeout, and wasted food. Meal Planner generates a full week of meals tailored to your household's size, dietary needs, skill level, and budget. It reuses ingredients across meals to minimize waste and gives you a sorted grocery list you can take straight to the store or send to Instacart.

## What It Does

- Creates a personalized weekly meal plan with recipes matched to your family size, dietary needs, and cooking skill
- Generates a consolidated, aisle-sorted grocery list that reuses ingredients across meals to save money and reduce waste
- Adapts to what you already have in your fridge so you buy less and throw away less

## How It Works

1. Tell the planner about your household — how many people, any dietary restrictions, and how much time you want to spend cooking.
2. Optionally list what you already have in your fridge or pantry.
3. Every Sunday (or whenever you choose), get a complete meal plan and grocery list for the week.

## Works With

- Instacart, Amazon Fresh
- Apple Reminders, Google Keep
- AnyList
