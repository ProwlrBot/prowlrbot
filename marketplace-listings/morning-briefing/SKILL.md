# Morning Briefing

Stop juggling five apps before your first cup of coffee. Morning Briefing aggregates your calendar events, unread emails, local weather forecast, and curated news headlines into a single, scannable digest delivered at the time you choose. It learns which topics you engage with and surfaces the most relevant items first.

## What It Does

- Pulls together calendar events, email highlights, weather, and news into one concise morning message
- Flags urgent items at the top — schedule conflicts, severe weather alerts, overdue tasks
- Learns your preferences over time and prioritizes the topics you actually read

## How It Works

1. Connect your email and calendar accounts during the quick setup wizard.
2. Set your location for weather and choose your preferred news categories.
3. Pick a delivery time and channel. Every morning, your briefing appears right on schedule.

## Works With

- Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar
- Gmail, Apple Mail
- OpenWeatherMap, NewsAPI
- Slack, Telegram, Discord

## Example Usage

```
Good morning! Here's your briefing for Tuesday, March 11.

CALENDAR (3 events):
  9:00 AM  Stand-up with engineering team (Zoom)
 11:30 AM  Dentist appointment — Dr. Patel
  3:00 PM  School pickup (reminder: early release today)

EMAIL HIGHLIGHTS:

  - Sarah shared "Q1 Proposal Draft" — awaiting your review

NEWS YOU FOLLOW:
  - Fed signals rate hold through June (Reuters)
  - New bike lane approved on Oak Street (Local Herald)
  - Anthropic releases Claude 4 model family (TechCrunch)

Have a great day!
```
