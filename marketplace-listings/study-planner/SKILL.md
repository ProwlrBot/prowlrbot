# Study Planner

Everyone knows they should "study ahead of time" — but nobody does, because turning a list of exams and assignments into an actual daily plan is hard. Study Planner does that for you. Tell it what's coming up, how much time you have each day, and it builds a realistic schedule using proven techniques like spaced repetition and active recall. It adjusts when things change and sends gentle nudges to keep you on track.

## What It Does

- Creates a personalized, day-by-day study plan based on your exams, assignments, and available hours
- Uses spaced repetition so you review material at the right intervals — not too early, not too late
- Adjusts your plan automatically when you miss a session or a new deadline gets added

## How It Works

1. Add your upcoming exams and assignment due dates (type them in or connect your school calendar).
2. Tell the planner how many hours you can study each day and when you prefer to study.
3. Get a daily schedule with specific subjects, time slots, and break reminders — updated as things change.

## Works With

- Google Calendar, Apple Calendar
- Notion
- Google Classroom
- Todoist

## Example Usage

```
You have 2 exams and 1 essay due. Here's your plan:

  4:00 PM  Biology Ch. 12 — Cell Division (25 min + 5 min break)

  Biology exam — THURSDAY (3 days away, on track)
  History exam — FRIDAY (4 days away, on track)

```
